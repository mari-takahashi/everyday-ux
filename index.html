PROFILE

MARI TAKAHASHI　高橋 真理

Born on 1986. Spent 10 years of childfood in Spain, Mexico, U.S.A. and Japan. Hobby back then was Manga and drawing. Once entered in a normal 4 years university, however could not give up the dream of becoming a creator, decided to study abroad for one year in Canada, taking art classes.
After graduating the university, started career as a web designer in Members, Co Ltd. (One of the biggest digital agency in Japan) Having experience in web design, IA through working with biggest Japanese companies, then transfered to the department which create new business. There, I learned process of UX and Agile development. After 2 years of experiences, again transfered to department to proceed agile development in fiduciary business.
Freelance from April, 2015. HCD-Net approved "Human Centered Design Specialist", TOEIC Score915, Top 5% of Most-Viewed on SlideShare.
